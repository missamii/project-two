About the APP Booket

Welcome to the book discovery app. Here you search for your a book. Find the price and get the ISBN number. You can also save the books you searched for to your account. If you have an certain Author that you liked from a previous book. You can search them in app to find other books written by that Author. And of course sharing is caring. You can share your favorite book with a friend. Just use the share button.
